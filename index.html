Hello, Welcome to Premier Sports Multiplex

Welcome to the Premier Multiplex Center, a sporting haven nestled in the heart of Corrales, New Mexico, where excellence in athletic facilities and opportunities thrives. Our state-of-the-art center boasts an impressive array of amenities designed to cater to sports enthusiasts of all ages and skill levels. With five dedicated courts for the ever-popular sport of Pickleball, widely regarded as the nicest courts in the region, you're guaranteed to have a fantastic time on the court. And that's just the beginning.

Step onto our full-size basketball court and unleash your inner baller, or dive into a competitive game of volleyball on one of our three spacious courts. But that's not all – for those who crave a diverse sports experience, our facility also includes a full platform gym, offering endless possibilities for athletes to train and develop their skills.

What we offer

PickleBall

The combination of premium surfacing, lighting, regulation dimensions, climate control, and spectator-friendly features makes indoor Pickleball courts exceptional places for both casual play and competitive tournaments. They provide the perfect environment for players to showcase their skills, build lasting friendships, and promote the love of the game.

Basketball / Volleyball

Incorporating a full-size basketball court into your facility not only promotes physical activity and competition but also fosters a sense of community. It's a place where individuals of all ages can come together to share their passion for the game, hone their skills, and create lasting memories on the hardwood. Whether for pick-up games, tournaments, or professional training, a full-size basketball court adds a dynamic dimension to your facility that inspires both players and fans alike.

Gym

Such a facility provides athletes with the ideal environment to train, develop their skills, and improve their athletic performance. Whether it's for professional athletes seeking to gain a competitive edge or young athletes looking to hone their abilities, a turfed athlete's gym with equipment like the VertiMax, sleds, and Olympic platforms is a true asset that can take individuals to the pinnacle of their athletic potential.

Kangan Water Station

Kangen Water, a term that has been making waves in the world of hydration and wellness, represents a remarkable advancement in the way we think about the water we consume. This specialized water, often produced by a Kangen Water machine, is celebrated for its unique properties and potential health benefits. It has gained popularity for its claim to offer not only refreshment but also alkalinity and antioxidant properties that set it apart from conventional tap or bottled water. In this introduction, we will dive into the fascinating world of Kangen Water, exploring what it is, how it's produced, and the potential advantages it may bring to those who choose to incorporate it into their daily lives.
