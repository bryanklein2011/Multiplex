Premier Pickleball

PSM presents an exclusive membership option that grants members the flexibility to reserve court time anytime during operational hours, provided courts are available and no events are underway. Members can enjoy the convenience of drop-in sessions, allowing them to engage in individual play or bring a group along, with the condition that non-members accompanying them adhere to the specified drop-in fee.

Additionally, PSM extends a welcoming atmosphere to non-members, inviting them to rent court ($25 a hour) time or join during designated drop-in hours for a fee of $10. This inclusive approach ensures that both members and non-members alike can partake in the sporting facilities, fostering a vibrant and dynamic community of sports enthusiasts.
